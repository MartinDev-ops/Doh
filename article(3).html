More Specialists Join Limpopo Health Team

The Limpopo Department of Health has welcomed a new group of medical specialists following a graduation ceremony at the College of Medicine of South Africa (CMSA). These professionals bring advanced qualifications and are expected to strengthen healthcare delivery across the province.

The newly qualified specialists include:

  • Dr. Makhubele – Paediatrics (Tshilidzini Hospital)
  • Dr. Masiya – Paediatrics (Tshilidzini Hospital)
  • Dr. Mojela – Psychiatry (Thabamoopo Hospital)
  • Dr. Monyeki – Psychiatry (Thabamoopo Hospital)
  • Dr. Mphahlele – Family Medicine (WF Knobel Hospital)
  • Dr. Motapo – Louis Trichardt Hospital
  • Dr. Mthombeni – Psychiatry (Thabamoopo Hospital)

This expansion of the specialist team is a major step toward addressing medical shortages and improving services in key areas such as child health, mental health, HIV care, and psychiatric support. The department expressed pride in the accomplishments of these doctors and emphasized their role in enhancing public healthcare across Limpopo.
