Two Underprivileged Young Women Receive Life-Changing Surgery

Through its Rural Health Matters outreach program, the Limpopo Department of Health has transformed the lives of two young women by providing critical surgical procedures at Mokopane District Hospital between 12 and 30 June 2023.

Tshireletso Thabane, 28, had a tumor removed from her jaw that had caused facial disfigurement and difficulty eating since 2013. She expressed deep gratitude for the surgery, saying it would help her regain confidence and improve her quality of life.

Thabiso Macheke, 17, underwent surgery to remove a tumor from her left thigh that had been growing since 2018. The tumor had caused her pain and difficulty walking. She shared her excitement about returning to school and living a normal life.

This outreach initiative highlights the importance of accessible healthcare for underprivileged communities. The procedures, which included advanced Maxillofacial and Orthopaedic surgeries, were made possible through the dedication of medical professionals and the department’s commitment to improving lives.
