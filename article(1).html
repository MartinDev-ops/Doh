Appointment Announcement: Dr. Ndoeni Norah Nthambeleni

Dr. Ndoeni Norah Nthambeleni is a seasoned medical professional and healthcare leader with a distinguished career spanning clinical practice, academia, and public health leadership. Her appointment as the Head of Department for Limpopo Department of Health marks a significant milestone in her journey of service and excellence.

Since 2012, Dr. Nthambeleni has held various leadership positions, including Acting Head of Department, Acting DDG for Tertiary Health Services and Academic Development, and Head of Clinical Department: Family Medicine at Mankweng Hospital. Her extensive experience in these roles has equipped her with a deep understanding of the healthcare system and the challenges it faces.

Dr. Nthambeleni began her career as a Medical Officer and Principal Specialist at Tshilidzini Hospital, where she demonstrated exceptional clinical skills and a commitment to patient care. Her transition into academic and leadership roles was driven by a passion for improving healthcare delivery and mentoring the next generation of medical professionals.

She is an active member of several professional organizations, including the Rural Doctors Association of South Africa and the Health Professions Council of South Africa (HPCSA). These affiliations reflect her dedication to professional development and advocacy for quality healthcare in rural communities.

Academically, Dr. Nthambeleni holds an MBChB, a Master’s in Family Medicine, a Postgraduate Diploma in Health Management, and an MDEd in Family Medicine. Her academic achievements underscore her commitment to continuous learning and excellence in medical education.

As the newly appointed Head of Department, Dr. Nthambeleni is poised to lead with vision, integrity, and a steadfast commitment to equity and health systems strengthening. Her leadership promises to shape the future of healthcare in Limpopo, ensuring that all citizens have access to quality and compassionate care.
